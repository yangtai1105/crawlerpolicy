## News

[OpenAI has launched a new academy resource focused on workspace agents](https://openai.com/academy/workspace-agents) that teaches users how to build, use, and scale agents within ChatGPT to automate repeatable workflows, integrate external tools, and optimize team operations. This offering represents a formal educational initiative supporting the agentic capabilities OpenAI has been rolling out across its platform since early 2026.

## Why it matters

This academy launch extends OpenAI's agent ecosystem strategy into hands-on developer and operator education, following its recent announcement of an [integrated agent and model suite for enterprise](https://openai.com/blog/next-phase-enterprise-ai) and the [Agentic Commerce Protocol launch](https://openai.com/blog/agentic-commerce). By providing structured training, OpenAI is reducing friction for organizations seeking to deploy workspace agents at scale—a critical enabler for the broader trend of ChatGPT-native automation that has dominated OpenAI's 2026 roadmap. The timing suggests OpenAI is prioritizing adoption infrastructure as agent features stabilize, signaling confidence in production readiness and intent to capture workflow-automation spending before competitors mature comparable offerings.
